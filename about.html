About Prague

This fairytale city is one of Europe's most beautiful cities, not only because of its architecture but also for its unique artistic sensibility. It has created incredible artists, authors, and filmmakers who have helped to give the city its distinct bohemian flavor. It has been compared to Paris on several occasions in this regard. Its magical atmosphere can take us to a different era. The city's old town, the Charles Bridge, and the Astronomical Cloc k are only a few of the treasures that the city offers to the growing number of tourists who visit each year
